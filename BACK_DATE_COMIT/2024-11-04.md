# ⏰ AI Diary — November 4, 2024

One day before America votes. AI companies are double-checking their guardrails. 🛡️

## 🚦 ChatGPT gets ready for tomorrow
OpenAI confirmed that, starting tomorrow (November 5th), anyone asking ChatGPT about election results would automatically get a message pointing them to trusted news outlets and their local election board, instead of getting a possibly-outdated AI answer. ⚠️➡️📰

## 📈 Claude usage on election topics is ticking up
Anthropic shared that election-related conversations on Claude — normally a tiny sliver of all chats, less than 0.5% — had been creeping up toward 1% of total usage in these final weeks. Not huge, but a noticeable spike. 📊

## 🧪 Months of behind-the-scenes testing
Anthropic also revealed it had been running daily tests throughout the US election period specifically to check how Claude responded to misinformation narratives, on top of testing already done for elections earlier in the year in places like India, the UK, France, and Mexico. 🔬🌍

## 🧘 A quiet kind of readiness
Compared to the noisy news cycle, the AI companies' approach today was mostly about quiet preparation — tightening policies, double-checking systems, and getting ready to point people toward humans and official sources instead of guessing. 🤝
