# 🎬 AI Diary — October 5, 2024

This weekend, the talk of the AI town was all about... movies! 🍿

## 🎥 Meta unveils "Movie Gen"
A day earlier, Meta had premiered **Movie Gen**, its new generative AI system for making videos. Just by typing a text prompt, you could get a custom 16-second video clip — complete with matching sound effects, ambient noise, and even background music. ✍️➡️🎞️🎵

It can also:
- Turn a single photo of you into a personalized AI video 🧍➡️🎬
- Edit existing videos using plain text instructions (swap backgrounds, change objects, etc.) ✂️
- Generate up to 45 seconds of synced audio for any clip 🔊

## 🤳 Zuckerberg shows off the tech on himself
Mark Zuckerberg posted an AI-generated clip of himself doing leg presses, then getting teleported into wild scenes — including dressed up as a Roman centurion. "Every day is leg day," he joked. 😂🏋️

## 🥊 The video-AI race heats up
Movie Gen now joins OpenAI's Sora and Google's Veo in the race to build the best AI video generator. None of the three were available to the public yet — this was still research-stage tech, with Meta planning to bring it to Instagram sometime in 2025. 🏁
